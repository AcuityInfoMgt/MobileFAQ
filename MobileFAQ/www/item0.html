Logging in for the First Time

Launch the Secure Sign-In application and login using your OpenNet username and password (Note: only the bare username is needed without SES and without @state.gov).

After signing in, you can press the Home button to exit the Secure Sign-In application.

Your OpenNet email, calendar and contacts information will begin to sync to the device. You will also be prompted to set a device password.

Next, sign in with your Apple ID within Settings > iTunes & App Store to ensure you can install applications from both Apple's AppStore and the enterprise AppStore (Apps@Work) as needed.
